VocaFlow Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how VocaFlow uses information when you use the app.

Information We Use

  • Microphone audio while the app is actively listening.
  • Recognized speech text for dictionary and translation lookup.
  • Locally cached word results on your device for faster future access.

How Information Is Used

  • To detect spoken words and show word meanings/examples.
  • To translate meanings into your selected language.
  • To improve response speed through local caching.

Third-Party Services

VocaFlow may send recognized text to third-party dictionary and translation APIs to provide app features.

Data Storage

Vocabulary and definition cache data are stored locally on your device. VocaFlow currently does not require account registration.

Contact

Changes to This Policy

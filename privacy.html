Privacy Policy

VocaPal

Effective date: 2026-03-24

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how VocaPal ("the App") handles information when you use the app. VocaPal is developed by Rokeya Akter.

VocaPal helps users practice vocabulary by listening to spoken English words and showing meanings and translated examples.

Information We Collect

VocaPal does not require account creation or login.

We do not intentionally collect personal identifiers such as your name, phone number, or home address.

Depending on your usage, the app may process the following data:

  • Spoken words recognized by device speech recognition
  • Word lookup text and example text sent for translation/lookup
  • Basic technical request data handled by third-party APIs (for example IP address and request metadata)
  • Anonymous lookup data (words, meanings, and examples) cached locally on your device and remotely in a shared cloud database to improve lookup speed for all users
  • Feedback you submit in the app (for example bug reports and feature suggestions), including feedback text, selected feedback type, app version, and submission timestamp
  • Anonymous product usage events used for app performance and quality improvement (for example analytics events related to practice, review prompts, and feedback submissions)

Microphone and Speech Recognition

VocaPal requests microphone and speech recognition permissions so it can detect spoken words and provide meanings/examples.

The app does not run background recording and only listens when you start listening in the app.

Notifications and Reminders

VocaPal can send optional daily reminder notifications to help you maintain a consistent vocabulary practice habit.

Reminders are scheduled on your device using local notifications. Reminder scheduling preferences (for example enabled state and time) are stored locally in app settings.

You can disable reminders anytime in app settings or your device notification settings.

Third-Party Services

VocaPal uses external services to fetch dictionary data and translations. These services may receive text queries and network metadata needed to process requests.

  • Dictionary API: dictionaryapi.dev
  • Google Cloud & Translation: cloud.google.com/terms/
  • LibreTranslate: libretranslate.com
  • MyMemory Translation: mymemory.translated.net
  • Firebase (Google): used for anonymous analytics, cloud dictionary cache, and submitted user feedback storage

Please review each provider's terms and privacy policy for details about their data handling.

How We Use Information

  • Detect spoken words and show vocabulary results
  • Translate meanings/examples into your selected language
  • Improve reliability and troubleshoot issues
  • Review and prioritize bug reports and feature requests submitted by users
  • Measure feature usage trends and app quality using anonymous analytics events

VocaPal does not sell your personal information.

Data Storage and Retention

Vocabulary cache data may be stored locally on your device to speed up repeated lookups. You can clear local app data by uninstalling the app.

Additionally, the text of definitions and translations is stored securely in a shared cloud database (Firebase) to provide faster, crowdsourced dictionary results. This shared data contains no personally identifiable information.

If you submit feedback in the app, that feedback is sent to a secure cloud database (Firebase Firestore) so the developer can review and improve the app. This may include the feedback text, feedback type, app version, and timestamp.

Local Backups: VocaPal offers an optional feature to export and restore your vocabulary list and settings. This process is entirely local. The backup file is generated and stored on your device, giving you full control over where it is saved. We do not transmit, collect, or store your backup files on our servers.

Children's Privacy

VocaPal is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect data processed by the app. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You can stop microphone access in your device settings.
  • You can disable daily reminders in app settings or in your device notification settings.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time.
  • You can choose whether or not to submit feedback.
  • You can contact us to request removal of submitted feedback where reasonably possible.

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
